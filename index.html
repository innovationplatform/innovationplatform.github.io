Haverford Innovation Platform

Get Started

HIP is a new initiative that connects thinkers, makers and doers. Jointly organized by Student Council and The Faculty and Staff, the Platform offers currents students with entrepreneurial drive guidance, mentorship, and structured learning opportunities in the form of a Workshop series, and forthcoming funding and networking services in the 16-17 Academic Year. For more information, email SC@haverford.edu.

In the meantime, click below to let us know where your interests lie, and we will help connect you with other talented students.

    Click ‘Makers’ if you have a skillset you would like to use to bring an innovative idea to life!

    Click ‘Ideators’ if you have an innovative idea that you’d like to see come to life

Meet the Team!

Ian Andolsek

Students' Council- HIP Coordinator

Ian is a senior philosophy major at Haverford College. Hailing from Portland, Maine, Ian is Co-President of the Students' Council. His interests in developing a student-led Innovation Platform at the College began after he spent his junior year as Co-treasurer of Students' Council with Tristan Pepin, and together they restructured the finances and bookkeeping of the Student Activities Fund to make the process more transparent, which saved money at the end of the Academic Year for use towards important projects on campus. His relevant interests are in media, music and visual arts and the spaces that house them, and organizational development.

Tristan Pepin

Students' Council Co-President

Tristan is a Junior Linguistics major and Computer Science minor at Haverford College. He’s from Waltham Massachusetts, and is co-President of the Student Council. His interests in developing a student-led Innovation Platform at the College began after he spent his sophomore year as co-treasurer of Student Council with Ian Andolsek and together they restructured the finances and bookkeeping of the Student Activities Fund to make money better available at the end of the Academic Year for use towards important projects on campus. He is working on a innovative project for Professor Schrier of the Chemistry department, the likes of which he hopes will be given more opportunities to flourish at Haverford and then grow beyond our campus through HIP

Nathan Sokolic

HIP- Special Advisor

Nathan is a sophomore economics major at Haverford College. He is from Atlanta, Georgia but recently moved to Ft. Lauderdale, Florida. He has a deep-rooted passion for problem-solving that has manifested in his entrepreneurial endeavors. Last year he was afforded the opportunity to take his interests abroad and develop a multi-national startup. His interests in developing a student-led Innovation Platform at the College began while he and another Haverford student constantly struggled with the pressures of simultaneously taking classes and developing a business without resources or advisement available at Haverford. This proved to be untenable, sparking his leave of absence to continue work abroad. He envisions a program that will allow students to meaningfully and effectively engage in both their studies and in projects they are passionate about. In his spare time he consults on the development of early-stage startups in the Philadelphia area and has been applying what he has learned through his endeavors to help refine the structure of HIP as a special advisor.

Dorian Wirz

Dorian is a senior psychology major and economics minor at Haverford College. Yue Xiang
